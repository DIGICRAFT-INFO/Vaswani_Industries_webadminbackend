Website Admin Guide

How to Manage Your Website Content

For: Vaswani Industries Limited — Management & Staff
Website: https://www.vaswaniindustries.com
Version: 1.0 | May 2026

EASY TO USE — NO CODING REQUIRED

1. How to Login to Admin Panel

1 Open browser and go to: https://www.vaswaniindustries.com/admin

2 Enter your credentials:

FieldValue
Emailadmin@vaswaniindustries.com
PasswordAdmin@2026

3 Click "Login" — You'll see the Dashboard

2. Dashboard Overview

After login, you'll see quick stats:

  • DOCS — Total PDF documents uploaded
  • MEDIA — News articles published
  • BOARD — Board members count
  • INVENTORY — Products listed
  • JOBS — Active job postings
  • INBOX — Contact form enquiries
💡 Tip: Red badge on "Notifications" shows unread alerts. Check daily!

3. Editing Website Pages

Go to Sidebar → Pages

You can edit content of these pages:

PageWhat You Can Edit
Home PageHero banner text, buttons, about section, products intro
About UsCompany info, Chairman's message, Vision & Mission
Our ProductsForging, Sponge Iron, Power descriptions
News & MediaPage header text
InvestorsPage banner, intro section
CareersHero, intro, CTA buttons

How to Edit a Page Section:

1 Click on the page card (e.g., "Home Page")

2 You'll see sections like "Hero Banner", "About Section", etc.

3 Edit the text fields (Title, Paragraph, etc.)

4 Upload images by clicking the image area

5 Click "Publish Section" — Changes go live immediately!

⚠️ Changes are immediate. Once you click Publish, the website updates instantly.

5. Publishing News & Media

Go to Sidebar → News & Media

Add a News Article:

1 Click "Add News"

2 Fill: Title, Category (CSR/Industrial/Factory), Content

3 Upload a cover image

4 Add tags (comma separated): solar, power, CSR

5 Click "Publish"

💡 News appears on the homepage slider and the /news page automatically.

6. Managing Board of Directors

Go to Sidebar → Board Members

Add/Edit a Board Member:

  • Name, Designation, Photo
  • Board Role Type (Independent Director, Executive Director, etc.)
  • Committee Memberships (Audit, Nomination, Stakeholders)
  • Social media links
  • Display order (1 = first)
💡 Committee assignments here automatically show on the "Our Committees" page.

7. Posting Jobs

Go to Sidebar → Careers

  • Add job title, department, location, experience required
  • Add responsibilities and skills
  • Set last date for applications
  • View received applications with resume downloads

8. Viewing Contact Enquiries

Go to Sidebar → Contacts

  • See all messages from website visitors
  • Click on a message to view full details
  • Click "Send Reply" to reply via email
  • Mark as read / Delete old messages
⚠️ Check this daily! New enquiries show as "UNREAD" with a red badge.

9. Updating Office Contact Info

Go to Sidebar → Contact Cards

  • Edit Head Office address
  • Update phone numbers
  • Change email addresses

10. Notifications

Go to Sidebar → Notifications

You'll get automatic alerts when:

  • Someone submits the contact form
  • Someone applies for a job
  • A page section is updated
  • A news article is published
  • A document is uploaded

11. System Settings

Go to Sidebar → System Settings

  • Site Name
  • Company Phone & Email
  • Address
  • Social Media Links (Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn)
  • Sister Concern details

13. Important Notes

Security:
  • Never share your admin password with unauthorized people
  • Always logout after use (click profile → Sign Out)
  • Change password periodically
Best Practices:
  • Upload images under 1MB for fast loading
  • PDFs should be under 50MB
  • Use clear, descriptive titles for documents
  • Check "Contacts" inbox daily for customer enquiries
  • Keep news section updated with latest CSR activities
